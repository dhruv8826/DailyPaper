Indian News

The U.S. sanctions waiver for India's Chabahar port project expires today, forcing the government to decide on the future of the 23-year-old strategic connectivity initiative. India's National Security Adviser Ajit Doval met with the UAE President in Abu Dhabi to review strategic ties and regional security dynamics. The India Meteorological Department has warned of a severe heatwave across large parts of Uttar Pradesh, with temperatures expected to remain high until April 28. Prime Minister Modi has condemned the shooting incident at the White House Correspondents' Dinner and extended well wishes for the safety of those involved. The National Investigation Agency (NIA) arrested a key accused in connection with a West Bengal bomb explosion case that occurred earlier this year. India's space sector is expanding as the government reviews plans to establish space laboratories in universities to build a pipeline of skilled youth. India records its highest-ever wind capacity addition in the current fiscal year, a milestone highlighted by the government as a boost for the renewable sector. The Indian women's cricket team secured a 14-run victory against South Africa in the fourth T20I, featuring a maiden fifer from Deepti Sharma. Factory fires and transport accidents were reported across various states, though officials confirmed no casualties in the major incidents. Political rallies continue in West Bengal ahead of the second phase of elections as major parties intensify their campaigning efforts.
