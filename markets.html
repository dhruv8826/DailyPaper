Share Market

Global stock markets are currently showing resilience as investors favor Big Tech stocks, which are outperforming amidst geopolitical tensions stemming from the U.S.-Iran standoff. The S&P 500 and Nasdaq indices closed at fresh record highs on Friday, bolstered by robust earnings in the technology sector. In India, equity markets continue to face uneven performance across sectors, leading to significant valuation gaps. The London stock market saw slight declines, with the FTSE 100 closing down as investors navigated a risk-off environment. Rising oil prices continue to influence global sentiment following production disruptions in the Middle East. Commodities, particularly energy, remain highly volatile as markets assess the long-term impact of supply chain shifts. The U.S. Federal Reserve is expected to maintain current interest rates in its upcoming meeting, a stance that has reportedly drawn displeasure from President Trump. Investors are closely watching ongoing ceasefire diplomatic efforts in the Middle East for signs of stability. Small-cap markets in the UK have shown a surprising, rapid snap-back after initial selling pressure related to regional conflicts.
