World News

Ceasefire negotiations between the U.S. and Iran are currently on hold after President Trump cancelled his envoys' planned trip to Pakistan for talks with Tehran. Iranian Foreign Minister Abbas Araghchi met with high-level Pakistani officials, including the army chief, in a continued effort to keep peace mediation efforts alive. Germany has announced plans to deploy naval units to the Mediterranean to prepare for a potential mission related to the security of the Strait of Hormuz. Local elections have begun in the West Bank and Gaza's Deir al-Balah, according to the Palestinian Central Elections Commission. Indonesian authorities are preparing to repatriate the remains of a peacekeeper who died after sustaining injuries while serving with UNIFIL in southern Lebanon. In Mali, a separatist group has claimed control of Kidal following reports of coordinated operations across northern parts of the country. Chinese Foreign Minister Wang Yi held talks with Myanmar's President Min Aung Hlaing, focusing on border stability and restoring the internal peace process. The 40th anniversary of the Chernobyl nuclear disaster is drawing global attention, sparking debates over energy security and the role of nuclear power in the energy transition. An international climate gathering in Colombia continues with ongoing negotiations centered on the future of fossil fuels and financing for green energy transitions. Israel has issued evacuation warnings for towns in southern Lebanon, citing continued military activity despite previous ceasefire extensions.
